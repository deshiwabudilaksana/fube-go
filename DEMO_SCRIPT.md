# 🚀 Project FUBE: The "Little-by-Little" Demo Script

This script is designed to take a restaurant owner or stakeholder from **"Cost Uncertainty"** to **"Operational Intelligence"** in under 5 minutes.

---

## 🛠 Pre-Demo Setup
1. **Database**: Ensure local MongoDB is running (`localhost:27017`).
2. **Seed Data**: Run the seeder to reset the environment:
   ```bash
   cd fube-go
   go run scripts/seed.go
   ```
3. **Start Engine**: Launch the server:
   ```bash
   go run main.go
   ```
4. **Open Dashboard**: Navigate to `http://localhost:8080/yield-planning`

## 🏗 Step 1: The "Non-Disruptive" Onboarding (1 Minute)
**Narrative:** *"Most POS systems require you to replace all your hardware. FUBE is different. We start as a 'Sidecar' to your existing Square or Toast system."*

*   **Action**: Go to the **Import** tab (`/import`).
*   **Action**: Explain the **CSV Importer**. *"You just upload your daily sales export from your current POS here."*
*   **Demo**: Show how legacy items (e.g., `POS-101`, `POS-102`) appear instantly in the FUBE system.
*   **Value**: **Zero Data Entry.** The system already knows what you sell.

## 🧠 Step 2: The "Yield Intelligence" Link (1 Minute)
**Narrative:** *"Square knows you sold a 'Classic Burger.' FUBE knows that burger just used 150g of Wagyu Beef, 1 Brioche Bun, and 20g of special sauce. Let's link them."*

*   **Action**: Go to the **Mapping** tab (`/mapping`).
*   **Action**: Find an **"Unmapped"** item (Red Badge).
*   **Action**: Click **"Link Recipe"**. Use the **HTMX Live Search** to type "Burger" and select **"Burger Prep."**
*   **Result**: The badge turns **Green (Mapped)**.
*   **Value**: **Immediate Visibility.** Click **"Export Costs"** and show the CSV. *"FUBE just calculated your exact profit margin for every item, ready to be uploaded back to your main POS for better accounting."*

## 👨‍🍳 Step 3: The "Kitchen Command Center" (1 Minute)
**Narrative:** *"Now that FUBE knows your recipes, let's see it in action in the kitchen. This is the KDS—the Kitchen Display System."*

*   **Action**: Open the **KDS** tab (`/kds`).
*   **Action**: Show the **Order Tickets** (HTMX auto-refreshes these every 5s).
*   **Action**: Click **"Start Preparing"** (Status: `PREPARING`).
*   **Action**: Click **"Order Ready"** (Status: `READY`).
*   **Value**: **Real-Time Inventory.** Explain that when the chef clicks "Ready," FUBE **instantly deducts** those specific grams of beef and sauce from your digital stock levels.

## 📊 Step 4: The "Leak Detection" Audit (1 Minute)
**Narrative:** *"At the end of the day, owners ask: 'Where is my money going?' This is our Inventory Audit."*

*   **Action**: Go to the **Inventory** tab (`/inventory`).
*   **Demo**: Show **"Theoretical Stock"** (what the system expects you to have).
*   **Action**: Update a **"Physical Count"** to be lower than expected (e.g., enter `5kg` when the system expects `7kg`).
*   **Result**: The **Variance** turns **Red**.
*   **Value**: **Waste & Theft Detection.** *"FUBE just flagged a 2kg beef shortage. Was it waste? Theft? Or over-portioning? Now you have the data to fix it."*
